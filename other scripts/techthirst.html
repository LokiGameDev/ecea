An Intra Department Technical Symposium

Technical Events

Paper Presentation

Showcase your research and innovative ideas to a panel of experts. This is an opportunity to present your findings on cutting-edge topics in electronics and communication, receive valuable feedback, and compete with peers from across the nation.

Project Presentation

Exhibit your most innovative projects and working models. This platform allows you to demonstrate your technical creativity and engineering skills. Present your work to a diverse audience and get recognized for your talent and hard work in building real-world solutions.

Coding Contest

Flex your coding muscles in a competitive programming challenge. Solve a series of algorithmic problems designed to test your problem-solving abilities and coding efficiency. Compete against the clock and other participants to claim the top spot.

Technical Quiz

Test your knowledge across a wide spectrum of electronics and communication topics. This fast-paced quiz will cover everything from fundamental concepts to the latest technological advancements. Team up and compete to prove you are the ultimate tech genius.

Non-Technical Events

Treasure Hunt

Embark on an exciting adventure across the campus, solving cryptic clues and completing fun challenges. This event tests your wit, teamwork, and problem-solving skills in a thrilling race against time. Gather your team and get ready to explore!

Connections

A fun and engaging event where you find the link between a series of seemingly unrelated images or words. Test your lateral thinking and pop culture knowledge. It’s a battle of wits to see who can make the cleverest connections the fastest!
